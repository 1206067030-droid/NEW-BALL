品牌故事 Our Story

NEW BALL 成立於對運動與時尚的熱愛。我們相信，每一位女性都值得擁有既能支撐高強度運動、又能在日常生活中展現時尚品味的健身服裝。 NEW BALL was founded on a passion for sports and fashion. We believe every woman deserves fitness clothing that can support high-intensity workouts while showcasing fashion sense in daily life.

我們的設計團隊由經驗豐富的運動服裝設計師組成，他們深入研究女性身形特點，結合最新的功能性面料技術，打造出既美觀又實用的瑜伽健身系列產品。 Our design team consists of experienced sportswear designers who deeply study women's body characteristics and combine the latest functional fabric technology to create beautiful and practical yoga fitness products.
